What we do?

At Greg, we extract facebook group posts, analyse its sentiment and record your post link for grievance redressals We can access/extract post from groups only if the said group is linked to our Page by the will of the group admin. We work in providing a better turn around time of the public's issues

Privacy Policy

  • We respect your privacy
  • We do not save or share your Facebook Account Details for any purpose
  • Only the post sentiment, link to post, set of hashtags used and tagged location is saved in our database
  • We are not paid by any government or private body in gathering any kind data.
  • Posting on said groups won't lead to any ad tailoring or cookies.
